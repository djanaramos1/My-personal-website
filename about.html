About Me Professionally

My name is Djana Ramos. I go by "DJ" for short. I was born and raised in Chicago. I attended Chicago High School for Agriculture Sciences. After graduating high school, I attended Moraine Valley College where I majored in Nursing. I served the healthcare industry for 20 years as Patient Care technician, Inpatient Coder, and DRG Validator, before deciding to give into my passion and creative side, and pursuing Software Engineering. I went on to graduate the Grace Hopper program at Fullstack Academy, and become a dedicated software engineer.

Some places I have been to include: Dubai, Cuba, Hawaii, etc. I consider myself a Disney Mom because we have been to Disney World, Disneyland, Disney cruises, and plan to visit all the Disney amusement parks around the world.
